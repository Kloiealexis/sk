NOTES ABOUT THE FOAM MACHINES: 
-The Dropper and Canon will make the same amount of space filled with foam, but the canon will actually shoot the foams.
      -The machines last about 60-90 minutes per session, but foam can last around 1.5 hrs-2 hrs after the machine is off if no wind and dependant on the amount of playing in the foam after off.
      -All you will need is a water hose and 1 outlet/extension cord.
